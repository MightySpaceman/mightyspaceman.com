IMAGES FROM SPACE

Image Credit Earthkam.org

Recently, I signed up for the Sally Ride EarthKam program run by NASA. the way EarthKam works is 4 times a year during mission periods of about a week, students are able to request images of locations on earth along the orbits of the ISS. A week and a half ago, I sent in my requests for many interesting locations on earth, and started receiving them a few days later. here is a showcase of all the images that I recieved. you can sign up for Sally Ride EarthKam at https://earthkam.org/

(note: the images may take a bit to load the first time)

I Do Not Own These Images. I was simply involved in the EarthKam program and this page is a showcase of my results. All credit goes to Earthkam.org and NASA.
