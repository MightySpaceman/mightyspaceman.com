About Me

Hi - I'm a Tasmanian programmer, author and I guess you could call me a youtuber; even if my channel only has 100 subscribers and I haven't touched it for the months that I have been writing my book. I live in Hobart, the capital of Tasmania, which is a really nice place, right on a river...most people say that tasmania is frigid and cold but, I can tell you, it can get warm (not that the rest of Australia considers 35 degrees particularly 'warm')

I'm currently writing a book about the future of space exploration, which will probably release somewhere in the next month or so. No guarantees though. At the time of writing I have finished writing it at 125 pages, and switched over to the editing process, which should take another few weeks. you can see updates of my progress on my Discord server. When I release it on the Kindle store, it will cost around $20 AUD. Buying it would really help me out but...shhhh...I've got a little secret for you...it will be free for the first 5 days.

MightySpaceman, my username and my YouTube channel, was always meant to be about space exploration. And it still is. For the majority of my life I have been a big follower of anything to do with space - and even if I haven't seen a rocket launch in person yet, space exploration is and will always be a lifelong passion. It's the main topic of my YouTube channel and, heck, I'm even writing a book about it now. I don't think I will ever lose interest in it.
